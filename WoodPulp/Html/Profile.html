About Us ____

Over 9 Years Experience in This Industry

  • Woodpulp, established in the year of 2015 with a best in class Lamination technology , an Initiative by a team of experienced engineers to serve woodworkers with world class products putting every sense of technicality into manufacturing. With a vision to promote Eco-Friendly lifestyle style Woodpulp brings you a range of particle board.
  • Woodpulp, actively involved into manufacturing of particleboard which adheres to international standard of Motherboard and Lamination as well.
  • Woodpulp Has 9’x6’ Mother board and Lamination plant with 200 CBM per day production capacity.
  • Woodplup always believes in Quality commitments and Customer Centric Approach.
